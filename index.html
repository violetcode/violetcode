Projects

  • DBT Diary

    An online DBT Diary Card

    This django application helps those with Borderline Personality Disorder (BPD) who are going through Dialectical Behavioral Therapy (DBT) to record their urges and emotions throughout their day. The site makes it easy to record, track, and then summarize useful data about DBT skills used, moods throughout the day, and other metrics.

    This was a django application meant to prank your friends based on the viral internet prank called CatFacts. Enter in a phone number, and it would text that number facts about cats every day. This is no longer being maintained, and is no longer functional.
